Our Team
Soubhik Chel
President
"We started our journey on 2014 as a little magazine to express our thoughts and to create a space for youths. Then with help of some energetic junior members we create our own youtube channel (Dristikon Online), website and Android application.

Our team also engaged with some social work, on (2017) flood in Tarakeswar, free career counselling program (2019), virtual puja parikrama (2020) during covid 19 (with help of tarakeswar Kendriya Puja Committee) , sent some fund to continue free Oxygen On Wheels program in tarakeswar locality (2021), and we also send 100 pieces sanitary napkin and some food packet to Sundarban after Yaas cyclone badly effected those areas (2021).

In my journey with Dristikon I learn where things are changing every day, will teach you to embrace and adapt to change rather than resist it. We Dristikonian are working towards perfection in several areas and we believe "Perfection is not attainable, but if we chase perfection we can catch excellence". "

Rupak Bid
Camera Operator, Video Editor
"I joined Dristikon as an avid camera op back in April 2019. Ya fellas, I completed the shoot and went in theatres for watching Avengers Endgame. Since then, I don’t recall how many projects I have collaborated in. Following which I got my camera, upgraded my PC, and now I’m into both; both into film-making and video editing. That doesn’t mean I never screwed up. ‘The Decision’ was one of my major projects, yet was an ultimate disaster; please don’t ask why! An older project is dangling just because I haven’t finished editing. And our upcoming project, I guess, won’t initiate without getting my kick-start. But yeah, that is why I love Dristikon - amongst the unprofessionalism, amongst the uncertainty, amongst the teamwork - that creates an opportunity of learning, and ultimately exploring the stuff I love."
